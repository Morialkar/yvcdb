# Phase 2 — Diagnosis

You are tracing the bug to its root cause. **Do not generate product code.**

Read `AFTER_BUG.md` and the failing reproduction. Trace the failure to its root cause, not a surface symptom. Document the causal chain, the exact location or locations responsible, and why the current behavior occurs. Consider alternative hypotheses and explicitly rule them out.

Do not modify product code. Record the root cause and the proposed fix strategy, with options, trade-offs, and any `REQUIRES_REVIEW`, in `AFTER_BUG.md`. Unresolved consequential choices are `DECISION_REQUIRED`. End with an approval checklist; the diagnosis must be self-contained.
